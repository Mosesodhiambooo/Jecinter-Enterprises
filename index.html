ABOUT Me

I am Jecinter Atieno, a proud fish trader based in Oyugis, Kenya. My journey began as a small fish seller, sourcing fresh tilapia, omena, and catfish from local fishermen at Rambira Beach. Over the years, I’ve worked hard to grow my business from a humble cart in the local market to becoming one of the leading fish suppliers across Kenya. Today, I supply fresh fish to market women in Oyugis, Kisii, Eldoret, and even Nairobi. My strong relationships with fishermen ensure that my customers receive the best quality, while my flexible payment options make it easier for market women to thrive in their businesses. I also serve as Chairman of the traders at Rambira Beach, where I advocate for fair practices and improved conditions for all. At the heart of my work is a passion for supporting my community. I believe in empowering women through business and continue to train and mentor young women entering the fish trade. My success is a testament to the power of hard work, determination, and the belief that anything is possible with the right mindset. Thank you for visiting my site, and I look forward to serving you.
